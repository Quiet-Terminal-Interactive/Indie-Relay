Promo types

A fixed catalogue of promotion types with committee-set minimum credit values. Requesters may offer more than the minimum.

Disclosure required: All promotional content must begin with "Ad: Indie Relay" on every platform used (ASA/FTC requirement). For livestreams, this must also be spoken aloud and shown on screen, not just stated once at the start.
Type Min. credits Quality bar
Social shoutout
Discord, X, Bluesky, Instagram, etc.		 1 cr Public post or message naming the game with a link. Must stay live for at least 2 weeks.
Community cross-post
Reciprocal shoutout		 1 cr Framed as the reciprocal "shout back", with the same requirements as the shoutout above.
Short-form video
TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels		 2 cr At least 15 seconds of footage or discussion of the game. Video must be public (not unlisted). Game name or link in caption or description.
Livestream
Twitch, YouTube Live		 3 cr At least 30 minutes playing or discussing the game. VOD must remain available for at least 2 weeks as proof. Disclosure must be spoken aloud and shown on-screen throughout the stream, not only at the start.
Long-form video / dedicated coverage
YouTube, etc.		 5 cr A dedicated segment of at least 3–5 minutes (or the full video if shorter). Must be public.
Written / press feature
Blog, outlet, newsletter		 5 cr At least ~150 words specifically about the game, with a link. Must be publicly accessible.

Credit rates are flat

A stream earns 3 credits whether the streamer has 50 viewers or 50,000. This avoids disputes over reach verification and follower-count claims. What matters is that the promo happened and met the quality bar, not how many people saw it.

Suggesting a new type

The catalogue is community-maintained. If you think a promo type is missing, submit a suggestion via the committee queue once you're a member. The committee reviews all suggestions alongside dispute queue items.
