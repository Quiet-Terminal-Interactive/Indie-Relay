How it works

Three steps, with no middlemen.

✦

Join the network

Apply as a developer, creator, streamer, or press member. Your account starts with 2 credits, enough to request your first short-form promotion on day one.

✦

Promote others

Browse the open request board. Stream a game, write a feature, post a shoutout. Submit proof when done, the requester approves it, and credits transfer to you automatically.

✦

Request promotion

Spend credits to post a request for your own game. Set your credit offer, choose a promo type, and wait for a member to pick it up. One request every two weeks.

Why this exists

Getting a review from a big channel costs thousands. Most solo devs can't reach audiences that aren't already theirs. Indie Relay routes around the money problem entirely. Credits have no cash value, which means no payment processing, no auctions, no pricing your reach against someone else's follower count.

No cash value
Credits can't be bought, sold, or cashed out. No tax complexity, no payment rails.
Flat credit rates
A stream is worth 3 credits whether you have 50 viewers or 5000. No follower-count disputes.
Community governed
Disputes go to the committee (active members, not an algorithm). Rules are reviewed as the community grows.
