Review promotion claim

A member has promoted your game and submitted proof. Check the link meets the quality bar, then approve to release their credits, or escalate if something's off.

What each action does

Approve
Credits transfer to the promoter immediately. The promo is marked complete.
Send to committee
Not sure? Let the committee decide. Treated as a routine review, lower priority than a dispute.
Dispute
You believe the promo didn't happen or didn't meet the quality bar. Goes to the committee as a priority dispute. Provide a reason.
